Why Should You Sponsor HackSate?

Our goal at HackState is to provide you, the sponsor, with three investment opportunities which each promise massive returns.


Invest in the future of Industry Leaders. Those that attend hackathons tend to be highly motivated and passionate individuals who look to revolutionize the way we live. Through your contribution, you gain the opportunity to recruit fresh minds and propel your business forward.


Receive valuable Product Feedback. Throughout the competition, students use technologies provided to us by our affiliates and sponsors to construct game-changing products and services. We encourage you to become a part of their process, see how they use your product, and get invaluable feedback on the services you provided them.


Experience Limitless Creativity. We operate with no boundaries or restrictions on the genre of development. Competitors come from all disciplines and bring vast experience, experience which will be used to develop some of the wildest creations you will see. We invite you to witness this audacious environment with us.


By sponsoring HackState, you make it possible to have a 24 hour experience like no other.
